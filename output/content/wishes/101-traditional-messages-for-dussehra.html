Adharm par Dharm ki jeet,


Anyay par nyay ki vijay.

Bure par acche ki jai jaikaar,

Yahi hai Dussehre ka tyohar,

Dussehra ki Hardik Shubhkamnayein!!

Dussehra means removing ten bad qualities within you:


Ahankara

Krodha

Swartha

Matsara

Mada

Kama Vasana

Lobha

Anyay

Amanavta

Moha

Remove the bad qualities of Raavana and instill good qualities of Lord Rama

Happy Dussehra !!

Happy Dussehra !!

May all your dreams come true,

Celebrate the Victory of The force of Good


Over Evil,

Lets celebrate an Auspicious Day

To begin new things in Life,Happy Dussehra !!

^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

May Lord Ram Eliminate your Sin,Evil and Illuminate Your Life with Virtue


Happy Dussehra !!


^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Dussehra reminds us of importance of Goodness,fighting injustice and following the


Righteous path

Happy Dussehra !!

^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

May Lord Rama Bless you by destroying the Devil in you


and fill your life with Sparkling Energy,Happiness,Success and prosperity.

Happy Dussehra !!

Let the Rejoicing of Festivity Embrace You and Your entire Family on this pious


occasion of VijayaDashmi

Happy Vijayadashmi !!

Buraai ka hota hai Vinaash,


Dussehra lata hai umees ki aas,

Ho aapke dukho ka bhi naash

Dussehra ki Shubhkamnaayein !!

Truth Always Wins,


Good always prevails,

This is the true essence of Dussehra,

Let's Celebrate and Welcome Lord Ram's Blessings,

Happy Dussehra !!
