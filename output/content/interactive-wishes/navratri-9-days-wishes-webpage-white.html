Navratri – 9 Days of Blessings

Navratri Wishes for Each Day

Share heartfelt greetings for every form of the Divine Mother. Filter by day, copy a message with one click, or print the whole set.

Day 1 • Shailaputri

Goddess Shailaputri

🌸 “May Goddess Shailaputri bless you with strength, courage, and stability on this first day of Navratri. Wishing you energy and enthusiasm to begin new journeys in life.”

Color: Red
Day 2 • Brahmacharini

Goddess Brahmacharini

🕉️ “On the second day of Navratri, may Goddess Brahmacharini grant you wisdom, devotion, and peace of mind. May your path always be guided by love and discipline.”

Color: Royal Blue
Day 3 • Chandraghanta

Goddess Chandraghanta

🪔 “May Goddess Chandraghanta remove all fears from your life and fill your heart with bravery and serenity. Wishing you harmony and happiness this Navratri.”

Color: Yellow
Day 4 • Kushmanda

Goddess Kushmanda

🌞 “On this fourth day, may Goddess Kushmanda bless your home with warmth, health, and prosperity. May her divine smile light up your life forever.”

Color: Green
Day 5 • Skandamata

Goddess Skandamata

👩‍👦 “On the fifth day of Navratri, may Goddess Skandamata shower you with motherly love, compassion, and protection. Wishing you peace and happiness in abundance.”

Color: Grey
Day 6 • Katyayani

Goddess Katyayani

⚔️ “May Goddess Katyayani bless you with courage to face challenges, strength to overcome obstacles, and success in all your endeavors. Happy sixth day of Navratri!”

Color: Orange
Day 7 • Kalaratri

Goddess Kalaratri

🔥 “On this day of Goddess Kalaratri, may all negativity be destroyed and your life be filled with light, protection, and divine blessings. Stay fearless and strong.”

Color: White
Day 8 • Mahagauri

Goddess Mahagauri

🌼 “May Goddess Mahagauri purify your heart and soul, blessing you with peace, harmony, and inner strength. May your life shine with her divine grace.”

Color: Pink
Day 9 • Siddhidatri

Goddess Siddhidatri

✨ “On the final day of Navratri, may Goddess Siddhidatri bless you with knowledge, success, and spiritual fulfillment. May you achieve all that you aspire for in life.”

Color: Purple
