Decorations

Red lanterns, paper cutouts, and couplets adorn homes, their vibrant hue signifying joy and luck while dispelling negativity.

Red Envelopes (Hongbao)

During Lunar New Year, married couples and seniors gift red envelopes filled with money to younger or single relatives, symbolizing good fortune and blessings for the coming year, not the sum itself.

Cleaning & Prepping

Before New Year, houses get a deep clean to banish past misfortune. Cleaning tools are then stored, ensuring good luck isn't accidentally removed in the new year.
