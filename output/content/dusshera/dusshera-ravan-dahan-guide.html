Building the Giant

At the core of the festivities lies the effigy. This guide walks you through the process of building Ravana's effigy, step by step. Navigate the tabs to explore each stage, from crafting the durable bamboo structure to adding the finishing decorative details. Every phase highlights essential materials and expert advice to ensure an impressive outcome.

1. The Bamboo Skeleton

The frame is essential, offering support and balance. Use sturdy, thick bamboo for the core and legs, while slimmer pieces work well for the arms and finer features. Bind all connections tightly with durable rope or wire.

  • Materials: Thick bamboo poles, thin bamboo sticks, strong coir rope, wire.
  • Tip: Build a broad, sturdy foundation to keep the effigy upright, even in strong winds.
  • Action: Build the torso initially, then connect the legs, arms, and neck. Verify all components are balanced.

2. Creating the Skin

When the frame is set, it's shaped and covered with a surface for painting, often using paper, cardboard, or stretched fabric.

  • Materials: Old newspapers, colored paper, cardboard, strong adhesive (flour paste), hay/straw.
  • Tip: Stuff the chest and limbs with hay or straw to add volume, then layer with paper for a more lifelike form.
  • Action: Layer paper with adhesive for added strength, ensuring each layer fully dries.

3. Crafting the Ten Heads

Ravana's ten heads are his defining feature. Each head can be crafted separately and then attached to the main structure. Pay attention to the facial expressions to make them look menacing.

  • Materials: Clay, papier-mâché, cardboard, paints.
  • Tip: Craft a central, oversized head for the middle, surrounded by nine smaller, matching heads. Use a lightweight material such as papier-mâché.
  • Action: Shape the faces, let them set, then coat them in vivid, striking hues. And of course, add the signature mustache!

4. Final Paint and Fireworks

The last stage involves painting the effigy to give it life and loading it with fireworks for the grand spectacle.

  • Materials: Brightly colored paints, decorative cloths, fireworks, crackers.
  • Tip: Adorn the figure in cultural garments and jewelry. Accentuate details with black and gold tones.
  • Action: Carefully place fireworks inside the body and head cavities, ensuring they are secure. This should ONLY be done by experienced adults.

Safety First, Always!

Ravan Dahan features fire and fireworks, placing utmost importance on safety. This segment details key safety measures to guarantee a risk-free celebration for all participants. Adhering to these rules isn’t optional—it’s vital for safeguarding the crew, spectators, and nearby property.

👷‍♀️ Construction Site

  • Ensure a stable, level base.
  • Wear gloves when handling bamboo/wire.
  • Keep the area clear of debris.

🎆 Firework Handling

  • Only adults should handle fireworks.
  • Store crackers in a cool, dry place.
  • Do not use damaged or dud fireworks.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Crowd Control

  • Establish a safe viewing distance.
  • Use barriers or ropes.
  • Have clear entry and exit points.

🧯 Emergency Prep

  • Keep fire extinguishers handy.
  • Have buckets of sand and water ready.
  • Know emergency contact numbers.
