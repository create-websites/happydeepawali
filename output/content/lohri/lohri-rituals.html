The Essential Guide

Celebrate the essence of warmth, abundance, and togetherness during Lohri with the help of this checklist, guiding you through every step of the festive preparations leading up to the bonfire prayers.

Celebrate with Purpose

This guide details each part of the evening, helping you uphold traditions while embracing the essence of thankfulness and unity.

Prepare

Gather items like Til, Gur, and wood before sunset.

Offer

Perform Agni Arpan and Parikrama with devotion.

Celebrate

Sing songs, share Prasad, and enjoy the feast.
