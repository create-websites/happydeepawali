Lohri Melodies
Heartbeat of Punjab

Songs of Fire & Folklore

Lohri songs serve as a spoken record of the past, honoring the tale of Dulla Bhatti and commemorating unity, courage, and appreciation with uncomplicated yet impactful lyrics.

More Than Just Melodies

These songs, sung during bonfires or Lohri collections, bridge generations to their cultural heritage, imparting lessons of bravery, kindness, and belief.

History

Honoring folk heroes like Dulla Bhatti who fought for justice.

Harvest

Expressing gratitude to nature for fertility and abundance.

Blessings

Showering love and good wishes on newborns and newlyweds.

Legendary Verses

The Most Famous Songs

1. Sundar Mundariye Ho

ਸੁੰਦਰ ਮੁੰਦਰਿਏ ਹੋ!
ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਵਾਲਾ ਹੋ!
ਦੁੱਲੇ ਦੀ ਧੀ ਵਿਆਹੀ ਹੋ!

Translation:

O lovely Sundri, who will look after you? Dulla Bhatti is your guardian, ensuring his daughter's marriage is honorable...

Significance: Acknowledges Dulla Bhatti for his efforts in safeguarding orphaned girls. Calls for blessings on those who uphold protection and social responsibility.

2. Dulla Bhatti Ho

ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਹੋ!
ਸ਼ੇਰ ਪੰਜਾਬ ਦਾ ਹੋ!
ਧਨੀਆਂ ਨੂੰ ਲੁੱਟਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਗਰੀਬਾਂ ਨੂੰ ਦਿੱਤਾ ਹੋ!

Translation:

O Dulla Bhatti! The Punjab's fearless lion! He plundered the wealthy and aided the needy!

Significance: Honors the defiance of oppression and upholds the bravery and righteousness seen in Punjabi legends.

3. Ho Tera Kaun Vichara Ho

ਹੋ ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਅੰਬਰਾਂ ਤੋਂ ਉਤਰਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਰੱਬ ਨੇ ਭੇਜਿਆ ਹੋ!

Meaning:

'Who will shield you? God has dispatched a guardian from above.'

Significance: Shows belief in divine protection and hope, which was crucial during harsh winters when survival relied on nature.

Nature & Play

Gratitude & Joy

Harvest Gratitude

ਆਦਿ ਆਉਣੀ ਲੋਹੜੀ ਵੇ,
ਵੇਖੋ ਜੱਟਾਂ ਦੀ ਜੋੜੀ ਵੇ...

Lohri is here, Witness the solidarity among the farmers, Present sesame seeds, indulge in sweet treats...

Expresses hope for prosperity and thanks nature for the harvest.

Children's Collection

ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ, ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ,
ਜੀਵਣ ਜਗਾਇਆ, ਫਸਲ ਲਿਆਈ...

Lohri is here, Celebrating life and abundance, Offer coins, sesame seeds, and treats, Or face the wrath of the flames!

Lighthearted verses promoting sharing, generosity, and festive fun.

Songs for New Beginnings

Linking Lohri to fertility, the continuation of family, and the joy of life.

ਪੁੱਤ ਜੰਮਿਆ ਘਰ ਖੁਸ਼ੀ ਆਈ,
ਲੋਹੜੀ ਦੀ ਅੱਗ ਰੌਸ਼ਨਾਈ
ਲੰਮੀ ਉਮਰ, ਚੜ੍ਹਦੀ ਕਲਾ,
ਵੱਸਦਾ ਰਹੇ ਇਹ ਘਰ ਸਦਾ

With the birth of a son, joy envelops the household, the Lohri flames glow brightly, May longevity and happiness prevail, May prosperity forever grace this abode.
