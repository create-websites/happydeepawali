Corporate Lohri
Workplace Culture

Warmth at Work

Promote unity and appreciation in the workplace by hosting a festive and inclusive Lohri event. Enhance team spirit and morale with a safe and engaging celebration.

Why Celebrate at the Office?

Lohri celebrations at the office don't have to be extravagant; even small activities can leave a lasting impression. They bring moments of happiness that break the monotony of everyday life.

Team Bonding

Fosters inclusivity and nurtures relationships among coworkers by participating in festive activities together.

Cultural Diversity

Acknowledges the varied backgrounds of staff members, fostering a positive and inclusive environment.

Morale Boost

Provides a refreshing escape from the daily grind, infusing vitality and optimism into the workday.

Professional Fun

Created to be both captivating and polished, with a focus on maintaining safety and showing respect for every employee.

Creative & Safe

Office-Friendly Ideas

7 Easy methods to infuse your workplace with holiday cheer.

Symbolic Bonfire

Construct a secure 'fire' with LED lights, paper flames, and decorative diyas for photo opportunities without the hazards of smoke.

Lohri Snack Counter

Arrange a table showcasing Rewari, Gajak, Popcorn, and Til-Laddoos, and provide information on their cultural significance for educational purposes.

Festive Playlist

In shared spaces, enjoy gentle Punjabi folk tunes such as 'Sundar Mundariye Ho' at a suitable volume for the office setting.

Traditional Attire

Promote the wearing of ethnic clothing such as Phulkari dupattas or Kurtas. Participation is optional to ensure comfort.

Quick Activities

Hold a brief 10-minute quiz on Lohri customs or a competition for desk decorations. Provide small treats as rewards instead of large prizes.

Photo Booth

Arrange a corner with items such as a dhol, phulkari fabrics, and signs for a unique touch. Perfect for inclusion in internal newsletters.

Goodie Bags

Provide small bags containing peanuts, popcorn, and a greeting card. Ideal for remote or busy employees.

Inclusive Celebration Tips

Explain the Meaning

Provide a concise explanation of the importance of Lohri for colleagues from non-Punjabi backgrounds to foster cultural

Keep it Voluntary

Allowing individuals the choice to participate in dress-up or games is important to ensure that everyone feels at ease.

Mindful Snacking

Provide vegetarian and allergy-friendly snack choices to accommodate all dietary needs.

Focus on Culture

Steer clear of rigid religious ceremonies and instead embrace the cultural festivities surrounding the bountiful harvest and cozy warmth.
